ABOUT ME

Welcome to my website/ online portfolio

Conor Claffey’s the name, I'm a 27 year old designer originally from Phibsboro, Dublin. I have been working as a designer for several years, most recently with Web Summit, a company that runs tech conferences worldwide. I have previously worked for the Sunday Business Post. I may be biased (definitely am) but am of the opinion that The Post provides the most rounded and independent media coverage in Ireland today. My work for the newspaper mostly consisted of layout design and illustration. Many of my illustrations have been published by the paper. Aside from this I do as many freelance projects as I can.

I am always looking to diversify my design work, having done work for both startups and multinational businesses. I also take pride in my personal projects; work that I do for fun. Apart from design I am a chronic sports fan, aswell as music, poetry and politics. I also have a keen interest in how the news media functions, with real fears for the future of ‘free press’.
